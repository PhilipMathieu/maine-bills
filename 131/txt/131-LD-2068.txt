1Sec. 1.  Definition.  Resolved:  That, as used in this resolve, unless the context 
2 otherwise indicates, "master plan" means the 2023 Augusta State Facilities Master Plan, 
3 the official version of which is held by the Department of Administrative and Financial 
4 Services, Bureau of General Services.
5Sec. 2.  Adoption.  Resolved:  That the master plan concepts for the East Campus 
6 and West Campus are adopted pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 302.
7Sec. 3.  Modifications.  Resolved:  That modifications to the master plan must be 
8 approved by the Capitol Planning Commission, as established in the Maine Revised 
9 Statutes, Title 5, section 12004-I, subsection 75.
10Sec. 4.  Consistency.  Resolved:  That, before a project is undertaken within the 
11 master plan area, the agency undertaking the project shall present an analysis of the 
12 project's consistency with the master plan to the Capitol Planning Commission.
13Sec. 5.  Outdoor lighting.  Resolved:  That the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, 
14 section 1769 does not apply to lighting on the East Campus or West Campus.
15Sec. 6.  Prior master plan.  Resolved:  That the master plan supersedes the 
16 previous plan adopted by the 120th Legislature in Resolve 2001, chapter 34.
17SUMMARY
18 This resolve facilitates legislative adoption of the 2023 Augusta State Facilities Master 
19 Plan, which replaces the 2001 master plan.
