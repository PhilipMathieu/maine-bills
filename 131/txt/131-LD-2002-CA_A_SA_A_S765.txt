fiscal year 2024-25 to the School and Student Supports program within the Department of Education for grants to 
school administrative units for contracted behavioral and mental health services for students and instead provides a 
one-time General Fund appropriation of $250,000 in fiscal year 2024-25 to the department for the same purpose.
