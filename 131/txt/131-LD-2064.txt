29SUMMARY
30 This bill amends the membership of the Marine Resources Advisory Council by adding 
31 the chair of the Aquaculture Advisory Council as an ex officio member and eliminating the 
32 seat representing the aquaculture industry selected by the Governor.  It expands the 
33 membership of the Aquaculture Advisory Council from 5 to 7 members, limits members 
34 to no more than 2 consecutive terms and expands the purpose of the council by requiring it 
35 to make recommendations on matters of interest to the aquaculture industry to the joint 
36 standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over marine resources matters.  
37 It also specifies officers for the council and increases the required number of meetings from 
38 one to 2 per year.  The bill amends the membership of the board that advises the 
39 Commissioner of Marine Resources on expenditures from the Lobster Research, Education 
40 and Development Fund by changing the membership structure from one that consists of 30
