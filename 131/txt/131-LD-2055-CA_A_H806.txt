8 SUMMARY
9 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. It makes rules regarding
10 electronic visit verification for providers offering hospice services under fhe MaineCare
11 program major substantive rules, prohibits the Department of Health and Human Services
12 from implementmg electronic visit verification earlier than March 1, 2025 and directs the
13 department by February 1, 2025 to report to the joint standing committee of the Legislature
14 having jurisdiction over health and human services matters on efforts to combat fraud,
15 waste and abuse in the delivery of hospice services, to include in the report infonnation on
16 federal initiatives that include M^edicaid hospice providers and to make recommendations
17 regarding the application of electronic visit verification to hospice services under the
18 MaineCare program. The amendment also removes the emergency preamble and
19 emergency clause.
