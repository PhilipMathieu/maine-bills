16 3. Allocation of pharmacy services support funding. Each federally qualified health 
17 center may apply for funds made available pursuant to subsection 1. paragraph D by

28 forth in subsection 1. paragraph D.'

1 SUMMARY 
2 This amendment transfers responsibility for administration and distribution of the grant 
3 program established in the bill from the Office of Affordable Health Care to the Department 
4 of Health and Human Services. The amendment also adds language directing the 
5 department to consider the availability of other pharmacy services in the areas served by a 
6 federally qualified health center applying for grant funding when making decisions to 
7 allocate funds to applicants.
