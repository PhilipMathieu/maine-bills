25 SUMMARY 
26 This amendment adds to the bill a requirement that $300,900 be transferred from the 
27 Fund for a Healthy Maine to General Fund undedicated revenue no later than June 30, 
28 2025. The amendment also requires, in fiscal years beginning on or after July 1, 2025, the 
29 State Controller, in consultation with the State Tax Assessor, to determine the General Fund 
30 revenue loss resulting from this legislation and transfer that amount at least annually from 
31 the Fund for a Healthy Maine to General Fund undedicated revenue.
