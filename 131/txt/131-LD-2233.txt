1Emergency preamble.  Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 
2 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and
3Whereas, the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A requires 
4 legislative authorization before major substantive agency rules may be finally adopted by 
5 the agency; and
6Whereas, a major substantive rule has been submitted to the Legislature outside the 
7 legislative rule acceptance period; and
8Whereas, immediate enactment of this resolve is necessary to record the Legislature's 
9 position on final adoption of the rule; and
10Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
11 the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 
12 immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 
13 therefore, be it
14Sec. 1.  Adoption.  Resolved:  That final adoption of Chapter 255: Workers' 
15 Compensation Fronting Companies, a provisionally adopted major substantive rule of the 
16 Department of Professional and Financial Regulation, Bureau of Insurance that has been 
17 submitted to the Legislature for review pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, 
18 chapter 375, subchapter 2-A outside the legislative rule acceptance period, is authorized.
19Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
20 takes effect when approved.
21SUMMARY
22 This resolve provides for legislative review of Chapter 255: Workers' Compensation 
23 Fronting Companies, a major substantive rule of the Department of Professional and 
24 Financial Regulation, Bureau of Insurance that was filed outside the legislative rule 
25 acceptance period.
