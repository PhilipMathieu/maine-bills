9 SUMMARY
10 This amendment adds language providing clarity that the Independent Commission to
11 Investigate the Facts of the Tragedy in Lewiston, in exercising its subpoena power, does so
12 in compliance with federal and state laws, rules and regulations and the Maine Rules of
13 Civil Procedure, The amendment also adds language clarifying thattestimony may be taken
14 in executive session upon a showing that confidentiality is necessary to the independent
15 commission's mission. The amendment adds a standard by which the independent
16 commission weighs whether to release testunony over the witness's objection. The
17 amendment requires the independent commission's cooperation with the State Archivist on
18 record maintenance, requires a report on use of subpoena power and establishes a sunset of
19 the independent commission's subpoena power on July 1, 2024. The amendment clarifies
20 that nothing in the resolve establishes a precedent authorizing independent commissions to
21 issue subpoenas in the future,
