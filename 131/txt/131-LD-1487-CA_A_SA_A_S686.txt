SUMMARY 
This amendment amends Committee Amendment "A" to make the following changes. 
1. It provides a definition of "authorized 3rd-patty provider" and amends the 
definitions of "digital electronic equipment," "documentation," "patt" and "tool." 
2. It specifies that the requirements of the right to repair provisions do not require an 
original equipment manufacturer that does not have access to documentation, patts or tools 
and does not offer services of diagnosis, maintenance or repair of pa1ticular digital 
electronic equipment to produce documentation, parts or tools for that particular 
equipment. 
3. It limits the exclusion from having to provide trade secrets to those trade secrets that 
are not necessary to perform diagnosis, maintenance or repair.
