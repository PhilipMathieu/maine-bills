15SUMMARY
16 This bill does the following regarding housing vouchers.
17 1.  It requires the Maine State Housing Authority to collect data regarding and to submit 
18 applications to the United States Department of Housing and Urban Development for 
19 waivers that:
20 A.   Allow the Maine State Housing Authority to enter into contracts at a higher rate 
21 than the fair market rental rate as established by the United States Department of 
22 Housing and Urban Development. The waiver may not result in fewer housing 
23 vouchers being issued than before the waiver was obtained; 
24 B.  Allow the Maine State Housing Authority or a municipal housing authority to 
25 establish reasonable time limits for the use of housing vouchers issued by the Maine 
26 State Housing Authority or the municipal housing authority; and 
27 C.  Allow the Maine State Housing Authority or a municipal housing authority to issue 
28 housing vouchers that may be used upon issuance anywhere within the State.
29 2.  Contingent upon approval of a waiver, it codifies under the powers and duties of the 
30 Maine State Housing Authority the power authorized by each waiver.
31 3.  It requires the Maine State Housing Authority to submit:
32 A.  Suggested legislation that requires all housing authorities or public corporations 
33 created or authorized pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 30-A, chapter 201 
34 to conform their housing voucher programs to the housing voucher program 
35 administered by the Maine State Housing Authority; and  
36 B.  A report detailing its efforts to obtain the 3 waivers from the United States 
37 Department of Housing and Urban Development.
38 The joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over housing 
39 matters is authorized to report out to the 132nd Legislature in 2025 legislation related to 
40 the report and suggested legislation submitted by the Maine State Housing Authority.1
