7 SUMMARY 
8 This amendment: 
9 1. Eliminates the Maine Emergency Medical Services Commission June 30, 2027; 
10 2. Removes the provisions concerning initial appointments to the commission and the 
11 terms the members must serve; 
12 3. Requires the Depaiiment of Public Safety to use funds in the Emergency Medical 
13 Services Stabilization and Sustainability Program to conduct the funding needs analysis; 
14 and 
15 4. Replaces the General Fund appropriation to conduct the funding needs analysis with 
16 an Other Special Revenue Funds allocation.
