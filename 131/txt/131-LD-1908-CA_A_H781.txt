Fiscal Detail and Notes
The Department ofEnvu-onmental Protection (DEP) has indicated that additional regulatory oversight will be required
to implement a prohibition on the use of certain restricted substances in cosmetics sold in the state. This bill includes
ongoing General Fund appropriations of $207,260 starting in fiscal year 2024-25 to the DEP for one Environmental
Specialist TV position, one Environmental Specialist IH position and associated position costs. The bill also includes
ongoing General Fund appropriations of $10,000 to the DEP starting in fiscal year 2024-25 for expenditures related to
the chemical testing of cosmetic products,
