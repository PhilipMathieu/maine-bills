24SUMMARY
25 This bill adds operating under the influence, or OUI, and operating or attempting to 
26 operate a watercraft, snowmobile or all-terrain vehicle, or ATV, under the influence to the 
27 list of crimes that do not qualify for immunity from arrest, prosecution and certain 
28 revocation and termination proceedings when assistance has been requested for a suspected 
29 drug-related overdose.25
