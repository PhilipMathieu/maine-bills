Fiscal Detail and Notes
This bill authorizes the Director of the Bureau of Parks and Lands (BPL) within the Department of Agriculture,
Conservation and Forestry to convey a small 0.34-acre parcel of Public Reserved Land to the Town ofWoodstock'"'
quitclaim deed, The land, part of 175 acres acquired in 2022, will be sold at fair market value and the revenue
received will be deposited to BPL's Oxford County Acquisitions Account to purchase conservation lands elsewhere in
the county.
