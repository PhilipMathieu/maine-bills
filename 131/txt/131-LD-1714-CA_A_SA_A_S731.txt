SUMMARY 
This amendment amends Committee Amendment "A" to remove from the bill the 
requirement that a portion of sales tax revenue received on the sale of adult use cannabis 
and adult use cannabis products and of the excise tax revenue collected on the sale of adult 
use cannabis be credited to the Recovery Community Centers Fund established by the bill. 
Instead, it requires that, beginning July 1, 2025 and annually thereafter, $2,000,000 from
