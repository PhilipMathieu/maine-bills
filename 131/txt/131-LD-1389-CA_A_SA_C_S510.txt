Fiscal Detail and Notes
This amendment eliminates the ongoing General Fund appropriations totaling $400,000 per year beginning in fiscal
year 2023-24 included in the bill to the Office of Innovation program within the Department of Education for the
establishment of a mobile learning lab for science, technology, engineering and mathematics and instead provides one-
time General Fund appropriations totaling $400,000 in fiscal years 2023-24 and 2024-25 only to the same program
within the department to be used for the same purpose,
The amendment also requires the State Controller to transfer $400,000 in fiscal year 2023-24 from the Department of
Audit, Unorganized Territory Education and Services - Audit, Other Special Revenue Funds account to the
unappropriated surplus of the General Fund to offset the General Fund appropriations in that year.
As amended, this bill includes one-time General Fund appropriations of $250,000 in fiscal year 2023-24 and fiscal
year 2024-25 for a mobile learning lab to allow for students in all areas of the State to engage in hands-on learning and
I one-time General Fund appropriations of $150,000 in fiscal year 2023-24 and fiscal year 2024-25 for contracted
staffing services to support the mobile learning lab.
