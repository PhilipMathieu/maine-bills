5 SUMMARY
6 This amendment replaces the bill, which is a concept draft, and changes the title and
7 adds an emergency preamble and emergency clause. It transfers $15,000,000 from the
8 unappropriated surplus of the General Fund to the Department of Administrative and
9 Financial Services, Property Tax Stabilization program. Other Special Revenue Funds
10 account to be used to fully reimburse municipalities for revenue lost under the property tax
11 stabilization program for senior citizens. It also transfers $50,000 from the unappropriated
12 surplus of the General Fund to the Department of Administrative and Financial Services,
13 Property Tax Stabilization - Mandate program to reimburse municipalities for
14 implementation and administrative costs of the property tax stabilization program. The
15 property tax stabilization program was amended in 2023 to apply only to the property tax
16 year beginning April 1,2023.
