32SUMMARY
33 This bill, which is reported out by the Joint Standing Committee on Health and Human 
34 Services pursuant to Resolve 2023, chapter 100, section 7, directs funds from the cigarette 
35 tax and the tobacco products tax to the Fund for a Healthy Maine.  Under the bill, tax 
36 revenue from the cigarette tax is applied to the Fund for a Healthy Maine only if Master 
37 Settlement Agreement funds and other sources are insufficient to cover budgeted 
38 allocations, and the tobacco products tax revenue is applied to the Fund for a Healthy Maine 
39 only if the cigarette tax revenue is insufficient to cover any remaining budgeted allocations.
