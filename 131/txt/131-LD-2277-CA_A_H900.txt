Fiscal Note for Bill as Amended by Committee Amendment '^V (|-(-'
Committee: Housing
Fiscal Note Required: Yes
Fiscal Note
Transfer of property
Fiscal Detail and Notes
This bill authorizes, but does not require, the Commissioner of Administrative and Financial Services to convey 3
parcels of state property by quitclaim deed, The property consists of 3 former court facilities located, in York County
each of which when the state opened the York Judicial Center in 2023, If neither the Maine State Housing Authority
nor a local public housing authority exercises the first option to purchase one or more of the parcels for residential
housing, the Commissioner may sell the property through a competitive process. Any proceeds from the sale of these
parcels must be deposited in the Capital Construction and Improvement Reserve Fund within the Department of
Administrative and Financial Services. The Governor's proposed supplemental budget for fiscal year 2024-25
includes one-time funding in fiscal year 2023-24 to maintain the 3 former court facilities in York County.
