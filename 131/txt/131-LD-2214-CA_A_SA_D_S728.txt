SUMMARY 
This amendment provides a one-time allocation of $3,800,000 from the Fund for a 
Healthy Maine to be distributed by the Department of Health and Human Services to a 
single grantee to provide management and oversight of the delivery of family planning 
services and comprehensive family life education. Additional funding in future years will 
be provided through a transfer of lapsing funds from the Depa1tment of Administrative and 
Financial Services, State Benefit Mandate Defrayal program.
