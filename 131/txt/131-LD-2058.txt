29SUMMARY
30 This bill provides that the Department of Environmental Protection may specify as a 
31 reason for nonacceptance of a permit or license application that the application was 
32 submitted after the activity requiring the permit or license has begun if the applicant 
33 knowingly violated a requirement to obtain a permit or license for the activity or the 
34 applicant previously violated a requirement to obtain a permit or license.
