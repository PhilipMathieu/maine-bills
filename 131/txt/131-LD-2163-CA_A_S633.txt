15 SUMMARY
16 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, replaces the bill and
17 changes the title. It does the following.
18 1. It provides that a competitive electricity provider may not renew a contract for
19 generation service without the express consent of the residential consumer if the renewal
20 rate is a fixed rate that is 20% or more above the contract rate in the expiring contract or
21 greater than the fixed rate that would, at the time of the consumer's renewal, be offered by
22 the competitive electricity provider to customers enrolling with the provider for an initial
23 contract of a similar term and product offering.
24 2. It prohibits a competitive electricity provider from renewing a contract for
25 generation service at a variable rate without the express consent of the residential consumer
26 if the expiring contract provided generation service at a fixed rate.
27 3. It prohibits a competitive electricity provider from renewing a contract for
28 generation service for a term that differs from the term of the expiring contract without the
29 express consent of the residential consumer.
