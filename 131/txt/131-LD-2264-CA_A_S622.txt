12 SUMMARY
13 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. It clarifies that a legislative
14 body of a town or village corporation may authorize the use of the town's or village
15 corporation's highway equipment to plow, maintain or repair private ways, otherwise
16 known as public easements. It allows the Maine Abandoned and Discontinued Roads
17 Commission to meet up to 12 times per year and directs the commission to consider the
18 scope of property owner liability and public use of public easements, property owner
19 liability on public easements, the benefits and costs of a road mventory and creating a right-
20 of-way template for property owners of land along an abandoned or discontinued road.
