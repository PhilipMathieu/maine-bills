8SUMMARY
9 This bill specifies that the mandatory minimum sentence of incarceration for a violation 
10 of operating under the influence applies when the person has operated a motor vehicle while 
11 having an alcohol level of 0.15 grams or more of alcohol per 100 milliliters of blood or 210 
12 liters of breath.  Current law applies the penalty if the person was tested as having an 
13 alcohol level of 0.15 grams or more of alcohol per 100 milliliters of blood or 210 liters of 
14 breath.
