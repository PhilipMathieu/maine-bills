Sec, 4. 30-A MRSA §4317 is enacted to read: 
§4317. Regional Council Support Fund established; rules authorized 
The Regional Council Supp01t Fund, referred to in this section as "the fund, 11 is 
established within the depa1iment's Bureau of Resource Information and Land Use 
Planning. For the purposes of this section, ''regional councils" means regional planning 
commissions and councils of government established under chapter 119, subchapter 1, The 
fund must be used exclusively to support the duties of the regional councils as outlined in 
section 2313, subsection 5 and section 2321, subsection 3. The fund may receive money 
from any available state, federal or private source, The fund may not lapse but must be 
carried forward to carry out the duties of the regional councils, The depaitment shall adopt 
rules establishing a formula for distribution to regional councils that factors in the number 
of municipalities each regional council serves and the population of the municipalities. The 
distribution formula may consider the nwnber of municipalities with which a regional 
council has entered into a contract for the purpose of certification of a growth management 
program under this chapter including assistance with ordinance adoption based on a 
comprehensive plan, Rules adopted pursuant to this section are routine technical rules as 
defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
