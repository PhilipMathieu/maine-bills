11SUMMARY
12 This bill authorizes the Commissioner of Environmental Protection to return as 
13 incomplete an application for a permit under the Natural Resources Protection Act if the 
14 commissioner finds that there is a reasonable likelihood that the proposed activity would 
15 be inconsistent with the minimum guidelines for municipal zoning and land use controls 
16 adopted by the Board of Environmental Protection under the laws governing shoreland 
17 zoning.
