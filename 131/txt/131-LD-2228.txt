2Sec. 1.  3 MRSA §902, sub-§1-E is enacted to read:
31-E.  Wabanaki veterans memorial.   The commission shall arrange for and oversee 
4 the development and implementation of a memorial in a portion of Capitol Park to honor 
5 the Wabanaki's defense and protection of their homelands and their service in the United 
6 States Armed Forces. The memorial must be consistent with the overall plan for the park 
7 under section 902‑A, subsection 2 and is subject to available funding for the park.  
8SUMMARY
9 This bill requires the State House and Capitol Park Commission to develop and 
10 implement a memorial to honor the Wabanaki's defense and protection of their homelands 
11 and their service in the United States Armed Forces.  The memorial must be in Capitol Park 
12 and must fit within the plan for Capitol Park and is subject to available funding for the park.9
