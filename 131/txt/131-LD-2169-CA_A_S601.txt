21 SUMMARY
22 This amendment replaces the bill with a resolve directing the Director of the Office of
23 Policy Innovation and the Future to develop proposals for potential financial assistance
24 programs to support the development of rental housing for persons making 80% to 150%
25 of the area median income and programs to support homeownership by persons making
26 120% to 180% of the area median income. The office is required to seek input from entities
27 familiar with programs that assist with the development of affordable housing as well as
28 commercial real estate developers, the Maine State Chamber of Commerce and the Maine
29 State Housing Authority.
