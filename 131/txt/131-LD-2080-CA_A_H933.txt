COMMITTEE AMENDMENT" A" to H.P. 1339, L.D. 2080 
SUMMARY 
2 This amendment adds a requirement directing the Department of Public Safety, 
3 Gambling Control Board to adopt rules relating to the conduct of advance deposit wagering 
4 that establish provisions allowing persons to restrict themselves from advance deposit 
5 wagering upon request by placing themselves on the universal list and that define standards 
6 for involuntary placement on the universal list and for removal from the universal list. It 
7 also directs the board to adopt these rules in a manner consistent, to the extent possible, for 
8 all forms of gambling included on the universal list.
