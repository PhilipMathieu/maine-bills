6SUMMARY
7 This bill is reported out by the Joint Standing Committee on Innovation, Development, 
8 Economic Advancement and Business pursuant to Resolve 2023, chapter 19, section 5 to 
9 implement the recommendations of the Department of the Secretary of State, Bureau of 
10 Motor Vehicles' motor vehicle dealer display area and licensing requirements working 
11 group.  The bill amends the motor vehicle laws regarding motor vehicle dealers by reducing 
12 the minimum size of the improved display area from 5,000 to 3,500 square feet for a 
13 building in the definition of "established place of business."
14 The committee has not taken a position on the substance of the bill.  By reporting the 
15 bill out the committee is not suggesting and does not intend to suggest that it agrees or 
16 disagrees with any aspect of the bill.  The committee is reporting the bill out for the sole 
17 purpose of having a bill printed that can be referred to the committee for an appropriate 
18 public hearing and subsequent processing in the normal course.  The committee is taking 
19 this action to ensure clarity and transparency in the legislative review of the proposals 
20 contained in the bill.7
