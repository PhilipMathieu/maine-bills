4 . SUMMARY
5 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, replaces the bill, which
6 is a concept draft, with a resolve. The amendment directs the Northern New England
7 Passenger Rail Authority to apply for funding through the federal corridor identification
8 and development program for the purpose of identifying the rail corridor that connects
9 Portland with Lewiston, Auburn, Waterville and Bangor as an intercity passenger rail
10 corridor. The Northern New England Passenger Rail Authority must apply for this funding
11 during the next available application window following the effective date of the resolve.

Fiscal Detail and Notes
The bill includes a one-time Highway Fund allocation to the Department of Transportation of $20,000 in fiscal year
2024-25 for consulting costs to facilitate the grant application. Any additional costs to the Department of
Transportation related to staff time to complete the application are expected to be minor and can be absorbed within
existing budgeted resources.
