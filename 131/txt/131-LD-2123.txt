13 practical evaluation  assessment of emergency medical treatment skills.
14SUMMARY
15 This bill amends the law governing the minimum requirements for licensing and 
16 relicensing emergency medical persons by removing the requirement that a person must 
17 have successfully completed the practical evaluation of emergency medical treatment skills 
18 approved by the Emergency Medical Services' Board and replacing it with a requirement 
19 that a person must have successfully completed an assessment of emergency medical 
20 treatment skills approved by the Emergency Medical Services' Board.
