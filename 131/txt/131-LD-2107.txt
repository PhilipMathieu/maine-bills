5SUMMARY
6 This bill requires that, when a criminal history background check of a person is required 
7 for the issuance or renewal of a teaching or educational personnel credential and that person 
8 was subject to a criminal history background check by the Department of Health and 
9 Human Services, the Commissioner of Education must rely on the criminal history 
10 background check information provided to the Department of Health and Human Services 
11 by the Maine Criminal Justice Information System and the Federal Bureau of Investigation.
