21SUMMARY
22 This bill requires the State Controller to transfer $3,135,000 from the General Fund to 
23 the Department of Health and Human Services, Nursing Facilities-MVH Other Special 
24 Revenue Funds account for payment to the Maine Veterans' Homes to support ongoing 
25 operations. The transfer amount must be increased annually by the increase in the cost of 
26 living. The bill also requires unencumbered appropriated funds for the Maine Veterans' 
27 Homes remaining at the end of each fiscal year to be deposited in the Maine Veterans' 
28 Homes Stabilization Fund.
