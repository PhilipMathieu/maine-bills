31 SUMMARY
32 This amendment adds a definition of "fulfillment provider" to the laws governing the
3 3 direct shipment of wine and authorizes a direct shipper of wine to use a fulfillment provider
34 for the direct shipment of wine. The amendment provides that fulfillment providers must
35 be registered with the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of
36 Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery Operations in order to ship wine on behalf of a direct
37 shipper to recipients in this State. The amendment provides that fulfillment providers must
38 also report quarterly in a similar manner as direct shippers and common carriers and
3 9 includes fulfillment providers under the record retention requirements provided in the bill.
40 The amendment clarifies that wine shipped under the laws governing the direct
41 shipment of wine must have been produced by or for the direct shipper, owned by the direct
42 shipper or sold under the winery name of the direct shipper in accordance with the direct
43 shipper's federal basic wine manufacturing permit.
