30SUMMARY
31 This bill is reported out by the Joint Standing Committee on State and Local 
32 Government pursuant to Title 3, section 955, subsection 4 for the sole purpose of turning 
33 the draft into a printed bill for referral back to the committee for public hearing and 
34 subsequent processing in the normal course of committee work.  The bill implements 
35 recommendations made as a result of the committee's review of the government evaluation 
36 report submitted to the committee by the Maine Municipal Bond Bank.  The bill requires 
37 the Maine Public Utility Financing Bank to submit a report by December 15, 2025 on its 
38 activities for a 10-year period to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having 
39 jurisdiction over energy and utility matters.  The bill also changes the requirement in 
40 current law that each special district in the State file an annual report with the Maine
