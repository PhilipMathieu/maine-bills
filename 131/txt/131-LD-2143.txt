28SUMMARY
29 This bill dedicates the revenue from the tax on the sale of electricity to the Low-Income 
30 Assistance Program administered by the Maine State Housing Authority to help low-
31 income homeowners and renters pay for electricity costs by providing a credit on the 
32 homeowners' and renters' electric bills.  The bill does not affect the current exemptions 
33 from sales tax for certain residential and manufacturing electricity uses.29
