3 SUMMARY
4 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, replaces the bill. It
5 changes existing law to allow a universal symbol to be affixed to an individually wrapped
6 or blister packaged edible cannabis product, and it identifies the following edible cannabis
7 products as impracticable to emboss, stamp, individually wrap or blister package: potato or
8 corn chips, popcorn, pretzels and loose granola. These edible cannabis products, and any
9 other edible cannabis product determined by the Department of Administrative and
10 Financial Services by rule to be impracticable to stamp, emboss, individually wrap or
11 blister package, must be packaged together with the universal symbol affixed to the
12 packaging,
