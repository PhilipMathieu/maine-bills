SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the resolve, adds an emergency preamble and clause and 
changes the title. It directs the Govemor's Energy Office to conduct a study of matters 
related to the State's future electric transmission infrastructure needs. It requires the office 
to coordinate with state agencies involved in the siting, permitting and regulation of electric 
transmission infrastructure and solicit information from a stakeholder group to assist in 
conducting the study. It requires the office to submit a repott by September 1, 2025 to the 
joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over utility matters that 
includes an overview of the study findings along with any recommendations. It provides 
that the committee may rep01t out a bill related to the rep01t to the Second Regular Session 
of the 132nd Legislature.
