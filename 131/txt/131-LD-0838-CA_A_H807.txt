Fiscal Detail and Notes
This bill includes a General Fund appropriation to the Office of the Chief Medical Examiner within the Office of the
Attorney General of $100,000 in fiscal year 2024-25 to procure services for forensic genetic genealogy testing of the
deoxyribonucleic acid, or DNA, of unidentified human remains, The fiscal year 2024-25 cost assumes testing of 20
bodies currently m the possession of the Office of the Chief Medical Examiner, In subsequent years the projected
cost assumes testing of 3 additional bodies per year,
