2 This amendment eliminates certain boards and commissions that either failed to file an
3 annual report with the Secretary of State in both 2022 and 2023 or filed a report that
4 mdicated the board or commission was inactive during 2022 and 2023, The amendment
5 eliminates:
6 1, The Advisory Committee on Fair Competition with Private Enterprise;
7 2, The Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics Council;
8 3. The Maine School for Marine Science, Technology, Transportation and Engineering
9 and its board of trustees; and
10 4. The Advisory Committee on Medical Education,
