This amendment eliminates the one half-time Labor & Safety Inspector position and the ongoing General Fund 
appropriations of $38,456 in fiscal year 2023-24 and $53,852 in fiscal year 2024-25 to the Regulation and 
Enforcement program within the Department of Labor included in the bill to support the anticipated increase in 
workload associated with ensuring compliance with the regulations regarding employer surveillance. The Department 
of Labor has indicated that without additional staffing resources it will need to prioritize enforcement activities.
