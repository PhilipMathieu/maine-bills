Fiscal Detail and Notes
This bill includes one-time General Fund appropriations totaling $100,000,000 in fiscal year 2024-25 to the Maine
State Housing Authority (MSHA) to support the production of mixed income housing and existing home ownership
assistance programs. Of that amount, $75,000,000 is appropriated on a one-time basis to a newly established
Community Housing Production Fund program for grants to non-profit developers, cooperatives and public entities for
the production of mixed income housing and $25,000,000 is appropriated on a one-time basis to the Housing
Authority - State program to support existing home ownership assistance programs. According to MSHA, it will be
able to implement the requirements of this legislation within existing budgeted resources and will not need to utilize a
percentage of the monies in the Community Housing Production Fund program to support its administrative costs,
