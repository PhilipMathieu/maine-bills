26 SUMMARY
27 This amendment provides that final adoption of the portions of Chapter 132; Learning
28 Results: Parameters for Essential Instruction for the content areas of science, technology
29 and engineering and social studies, a provisionally adopted major substantive rule of the
30 Department of Education that has been submitted to the Legislature for review pursuant to
31 the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A, is not authorized.
32 The amendment also directs the Department of Education to initiate rulemaking on the
33 social studies portion of Chapter 132: Learning Results: Parameters for Essential
34 Instruction and to submit the provisionally adopted rule to the Legislature in the First
35 Regular Session of the 132nd Legislature for review. The department is authorized to adjust
