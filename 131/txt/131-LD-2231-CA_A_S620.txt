Fiscal Note
Potential revenue loss - University of Maine System
Fiscal Detail and Notes
Requiring the University of Maine System (UMS) to convey without compensation to the City of Belfast the UMS's
interest in the land and any buildings, including the building commonly known as the University of Maine Hutchipc"-*n
Center, may result in a potential loss of revenue to the UMS that could have been received from leasing or selling
property. The current appraised value of the building and land is $2.52 million. Additionally, the UMS owes
$885,000 on a revenue bond taken out to finance an expansion of the facility that was completed in 2009. According
to the UMS, it has initiated a competitive request for proposal seeking purchase, lease or creative alternative offers for
the center.
