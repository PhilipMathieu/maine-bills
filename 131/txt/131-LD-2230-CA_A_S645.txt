11 SUMMARY
12 This amendment replaces the bill with a resolve and adds an emergency preamble and
13 clause. It requires the Department of Health and Human Services to convene a stakeholder
14 group to participate in the development of proposals to amend its rule Chapter 208: Rules
15 Relating to Boys, Girls, Boys and Girls, Day Camps and Primitive and Trip Camping prior
16 to publishing a rule change for public comment and adoption under the Maine
17 Administrative Procedure Act. The department may not adopt any rules to amend its rule
18 Chapter 208 until the department has convened and engaged a stakeholder group.
