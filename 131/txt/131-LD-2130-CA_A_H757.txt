'orrectional and Judicial Impact Statements
The bill creates a Class D crime for unauthorized paramilitary training,
This bill may increase the number of new cases and civil suits filed in the court system,
The additional workload associated with the minimal number of cases filed in the court system does not require
additional funding at this time,
The collection of additional fines and filing fees will increase General Fund or other dedicated revenue by minor
amounts.
Fiscal Detail and Notes
Authorizing the Attorney General to bring a civil action to restrain or prevent unauthorized paramilitary training may
result in additional costs to the Office of the Attorney General which are expected to be minor and can be absorbed
within existing budgeted resources.
