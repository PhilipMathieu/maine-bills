13 SUMMARY
14 This amendment, which replaces the bill with a resolve, directs the Department of
15 Inland Fisheries and Wildlife and the Department of Marine Resources to enter into a
16 memorandum of understanding to establish a process to plan for actions to control and
17 prevent further spread of aquatic invasive species as fish passages are improved and fish
18 barriers are removed statewide. The amendment directs the departments to include a
19 requirement in the memorandum that the memorandum be updated at least every 5 years
20 and that the departments provide notice to the joint standing committee of the Legislature
21 having jurisdiction over inland fisheries and wildlife matters any time the memorandum is
22 reviewed and updated. The amendment also directs the departments to include a
23 requirement in the memorandum that if changes are planned that reduce commitments
24 outlined in the memorandum to control and prevent the further spread of aquatic invasive
25 species at the dam on the Penobscot River in the Town of Medway or the dam on the
26 Piscataquis River at Brown's M^ill in the Town of Dover-Foxcroft, the jomt standing
27 committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over inland fisheries and wildlife matters
28 must be notified in writing prior to the changes being implemented.
