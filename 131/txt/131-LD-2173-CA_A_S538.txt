9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT " /-\" to S.P. 924, L.D. 2173, "Resolve, to Rename
10 the Interstate 295 Interchange in the Town of Freeport the Matthew MacMillan Bridge"
11 Amend the resolve by inserting before section 1 the followmg:
12 'Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not
13 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and
14 Whereas, Resolve 2023, chapter 1 named an overpass in the Town ofFreeport for
15 Matthew MacMUlan; and
16 Whereas, the incorrect structure was named after Matthew MacMillan and this
17 legislation corrects that erroneous designation; and
18 Whereas, it is imperative that this legislation take effect as soon as possible in order
19 to timely expedite the renaming process; and
20 Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within
21 the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as
22 immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now,
23 therefore,'
24 Amend the resolve by adding before the summary the following:
25 'Emergency clause. In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation
26 takes effect when approved.'
27 Amend the resolve by relettermg or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or
28 section number to read consecutively.
29 SUMMARY
30 This amendment adds an emergency preamble and emergency clause to the resolve.
