15 SUMMARY
16 This amendment changes the title of and removes from the resolve the provisions that
17 direct the Department of Health and Human Services to amend reimbursement rates for
18 private nonmedical institutions that receive reimbursement under the MaineCare Benefits
19 Manual in Chapter III, Section 97, Appendices E and F and Chapters II and III, Section 65.
20 It also removes the portion of the resolve that establishes a stakeholder group. It retains
21 the provision that requires timely reimbursement to those private nonmedical institutions
22 by the department. The amendment requires the department to increase awareness of the
23 rate adjustment application process that exists for those private nonmedical institutions and
24 provides $2,000,000 for increased rate adjustments.

Fiscal Detail and Notes
This bill includes a one-time General Fund appropriation to the Department of Health and Human Services of
$741,849 in fiscal year 2024-25 to support rate adjustments to eligible private nonmedical institutions that receive
reimbursement under Chapter 101, MaineCare Benefits Manual, Section 97, Appendix E and Appendix F. A one-time
Federal Expenditures Fund allocation is also included for the FMAP match.
