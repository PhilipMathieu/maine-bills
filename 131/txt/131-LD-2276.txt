Fiscal Detail and Notes
Fiscal Note
This bill increases the amount of gasoline taxes transferred from the Highway Fund to various snowmobile and ATV 
funds within the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Foresty (DACF). It results in a reduction in Highway 
Fund revenue of $705,914, a decrease in General Fund revenue of $58 and a reduction in TransCap Trust Fund 
revenue of $80,620 in fiscal year 2024-25.  It also reduces Other Special Revenue Funds allocations of $80,620 in 
fiscal year 2024-25.  The bill includes allocations of $786,605 to the DACF in fiscal year 2024-25 and a deallocation 
of $13 to the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife in fiscal year 2024-25.
