SUMMARY 
This amendment specifies that the harmony agreement is binding on the facility owner 
or operator, as well as employers, employees and contractors and subcontractors, as 
specified in Committee Amendment "A". The amendment requires the Department of 
Labor to establish a definition of "temporary basis" for purposes of excepting employees 
of contractors and subcontractors from the harmony agreement. The amendment provides 
a definition of "maintenance" for purposes of the harmony agreement and excepts services 
that are upgrades performed by a 3rd party. The amendment requires any manufacturing 
done on a site covered by a harmony agreement to be for the purpose of supplying a clean 
energy development project with goods and materials for the operation of the project.
