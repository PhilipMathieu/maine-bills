Fiscal Detail and Notes
This bill-includes ongoing General Fund appropriations totaling $650,000 per year beginning in fiscal year 2024-25 to
the General Purpose Aid for Local Schools program within the Department of Education to provide funding for the
Maine School of Science and Mathematics. Of that amount, $400,000 is appropriated to support the administrative
operations of the school and $250,000 is appropriated for financial support to in-state students who demonstrate a
need for room and board expenses.
