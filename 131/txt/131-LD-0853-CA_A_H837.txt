1 Sec. 2. Report. Resolved: That, no later than January 15,2025, the department shall
2 submit a report to the joint standing or joint select committee of the Legislature having
3 jurisdiction over housing matters with a proposed amendment to the Constitution of Maine
4 establishing a right to housing that directs the Legislature to enact appropriate laws to
5 execute the purposes of the amendment. The report must describe how the proposed
6 constitutional amendment will ensure fair access to housing for all residents of the State

11 SUMMARY
12 This amendment replaces the resolution with a resolve directing the Department of
13 Economic and Community Development to convene a stakeholder group to develop a
14 proposed amendment to the Constitution ofMame establishing a right to housing that also
15 directs the Legislature to enact appropriate laws to execute the amendment. The
16 department is required to submit a report with the proposed amendment to the joint standing
17 or joint select committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over housing matters no
18 later than January 15, 2025, and the committee is authorized to submit legislation to the
19 132nd Legislature in 2025.
