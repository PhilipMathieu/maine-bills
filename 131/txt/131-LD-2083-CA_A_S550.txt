11 SUMMARY
12 This amendment replaces the resolve. It directs the Department of Health and Human
13 Services to convene a stakeholder group to study access to psychiatric medication
14 management services in the State. The study must examine the availability of psychiatric
15 medication management services across the State, including a review of geographic and
16 other disparities in access, the availability of psychiatric nurse practitioners to provide
17 medication management and challenges to sustaining and expanding the psychiatric nurse
18 practitioner workforce, including an examination of educational opportunities, financial
19 support for the education of nurse practitioners and the availability of nurse educators to
20 train nurse practitioners. It directs the department to report to the joint standing committee
21 of the Legislature having jurisdiction over health and human services matters no later than
22 February 1, 2025 on the study. The joint standing committee may report out legislation
23 related to the report to the 132nd Legislature in 2025.
