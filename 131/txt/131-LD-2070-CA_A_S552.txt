12 SUMMARY
13 This amendment does the following.
14 1. The bill provides that a facility-based monitoring system is used to disable slot
15 machines; the amendment instead provides that the Department of Public Safety, Gambling
16 Control Board, or the director or staff of the board, in collaboration with a casino operator
17 or slot machine operator, has the ability to disable slot machines when appropriate.
18 2. It clarifies that an on-site computer system that is accessible by the Department of
19 Public Safety through read-only access and was in operation as of January 1, 2024 at a
20 casino or slot machine facility licensed by the State is considered a facility-based
21 monitoring system.
22 3. It amends the provision of law regarding examination of slot machines and
23 associated equipment to provide that the Gambling Control Board, in cooperation with the
24 Department of Public Safety, must approve qualified independent laboratories for
25 certification of slot machines and associated equipment from slot machine distributors and
26 gambling services vendors seeking registration. A slot machine distributor or gambling
27 services vendor seeking certification of the slot machine or associated equipment must pay
28 the board-approved independent laboratory the cost of the certification before it is
29 completed. The Gambling Control Board may adopt routine technical rules to establish the
30 approval process to verify certifications, approve shipments and inspect slot machines.
31 4. It adds an emergency preamble and emergency clause and an effective date of June
32 30,2024.

Fiscal Detail and Notes
This bill replaces the current requirement that the Gambling Control Unit (GCU) within the Department of Public
Safety maintain a central site monitoring system with a requirement that all slot machines be able to electronically
communicate with the GCU starting June 30, 2024. The bill includes a deallocation of Other Special Revenue Funds
of $500,000 in fiscal year 2024-25 to reflect elimination of expenses related to the maintenance of the central site
monitoring system.
