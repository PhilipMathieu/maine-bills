SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, replaces the bill. It 
amends the automotive right to repair law as follows. 
1. It provides additional definitions, 
2, It further defines the types of services that are considered patt of a telematics system. 
3. It removes the provision that provides that manufacturers are not permitted to 
require authorization before providing access to a vehicle's on-board diagnostic systems. 
4. It repeals the provisions establishing and providing enforcement authority to an 
independent entity and establishes pi'ocesses by which independent repafr facilities, owners 
or the Attorney General may enfotce the law against manufacturers. 
5. It repeals the provision regarding required equipment for accessing vehicle­
generated data. 
6. It stipulates the limited applicability of the law to mobile communications. 
7. It adds provisions regarding the interpretation of the law.
