3ยง13056-K.  Workforce Housing Development Loan Fund
41.  Fund established.  The Workforce Housing Development Loan Fund, referred to 
5 in this section as "the fund," is established as a revolving, nonlapsing fund within the 
6 department to provide loans to support the development of affordable workforce housing.  
7 The fund consists of money appropriated to the fund by the Legislature and any funds 
8 received by the department for the purposes of the fund.
92.  Use of fund.  The department shall solicit applications for loans from the fund 
10 through a competitive application process.  Loans may be awarded to community banks at 
11 an interest rate of 0% to provide funding to housing developers to support the development 
12 of workforce housing that is affordable for employees who earn 60% to 120% of the area 
13 median income as determined by the United States Department of Housing and Urban 
14 Development.
153.  Fund evaluation.  A recipient of a loan through the fund shall cooperate with the 
16 department in the department's evaluation process and with respect to specific reporting 
17 requirements.
184.  Rules.  The department shall adopt routine technical rules pursuant to chapter 375, 
19 subchapter 2-A to carry out the purposes of this section.
20SUMMARY
21 This bill establishes the Workforce Housing Development Loan Fund in the 
22 Department of Economic and Community Development to provide loans to support the 
23 development of affordable workforce housing.  The department must solicit applications 
24 for loans from the fund through a competitive application process.  Loans may be awarded 
25 to community banks at an interest rate of 0% to provide funding to housing developers to 
26 develop housing for employees who earn 60% to 120% of the area median income as 
27 determined by the United States Department of Housing and Urban Development.
