Fiscal Detail and Notes
This bill includes a one-time Other Special Revenue Funds allocation of $7,500,000 in fiscal year 2024-25 to the
Child Development Services (CDS) program within the Department of Education for payments for specially designed
instruction provided by each special purpose private preschool at a rate of $125 per day, per child receiving medically
necessaiy therapeutic services through the MaineCare program for scheduled school days m accordance with a child's
individualized education plan. The bill also provides for a one-time $7,500,000 transfer from the unappropriated
surplus of the General Fund to CDS to provide funding for the payments,
