9 SUMMARY
10 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. The amendment provides that
11 the Public Utilities Commission may periodically review and alter, by major substantive
12 mle, the amount of compensation that a customer with a financial interest in a distributed
13 generation resource receives as a result of participating in the tariff rate net energy billing
14 program under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 35-A, section 3209-B.
