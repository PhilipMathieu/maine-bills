19 SUMMARY 
20 This amendment is the minority rep01t of the committee, The amendment amends a 
21 provision of law governing testing for HIV without consent to describe a source patient as 
22 an "incapacitated adult" and also clarifies that an HIV test may be conducted without 
23 consent when there is not a person available within 8 hours of the exposure who has legal 
24 authority to consent in time for the person who is exposed to receive appropriate medical 
25 treatment.
