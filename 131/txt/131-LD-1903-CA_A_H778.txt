Fiscal Detail and Notes
This bill includes one-time appropriations of $191,570 in fiscal year 2024-25 for costs associated with conducting a
technical review and revision of the Maine Criminal Code and other statutes defining crimes in the Maine Revised
Statutes, This total includes appropriations to the Department of the Attorney General of $185,200 for consulting
services and $2,520 for reimbursement of expenses of public members of the advisory council created to oversee the
review. It also includes appropriations of $3,850 to the Judicial Department for the costs of a retired judge or justice
serving on the advisory council,
