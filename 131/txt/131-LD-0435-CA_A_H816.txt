7 SUMMARY
8 This amendment replaces the resolve and changes the title. It requires the Department
9 of Health and Human Services to review relevant data and conduct an analysis on the
10 capacity, occupancy and availability of and access to children's residential treatment
11 services in the State and the residential treatment services located outside of the State that
12 are being provided to children who normally reside in this State. By January 2, 2025, the
13 department must submit to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having
14 jurisdiction over health and human services matters a report summarizmg the data and
15 results of the analysis of children s residential treatment services, an update on the
16 department's progress in implementing its strategic priorities developed in 2019 to
17 implement the children's behavioral health services plan for Maine developed by the
18 department's Office of Child and Family Services and a summary of the department's
19 current priorities to ensure the availability, quality and consistency of and access to
20 behavioral health care services for children.
