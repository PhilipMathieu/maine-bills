15 'Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not
16 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and
17 Whereas, the Monhegan Water Company was created by Private and Special Law
18 1913, chapter 35 for the purpose of supplying Monhegan Plantation in the county of
19 Lincoln and the inhabitants of the plantation with pure water for domestic, sanitary,
20 commercial, industrial, municipal and public purposes; and
21 Whereas, the Monhegan Water Company presently provides water service to the
22 residents of Monhegan Plantation; and
23 Whereas, at its 2023 annual meeting on April 8, 2023, the voters of Monhegan
24 Plantation voted in favor of warrant article 32, which asked whether the plantation wanted
25 to vote to dissolve the Monhegan Water Company and transfer the company's assets to
26 Monhegan Plantation; and
27 Whereas, this legislation provides for the transfer of the functions and assets of the
28 Monhegan Water Company to Monhegan Plantation for the purpose of establishing and
29 operating the Monhegan Plantation water district owned, controlled, managed and operated
30 by Monhegan Plantation; and
31 Whereas, the Monhegan Water Company and the Monhegan Plantation seek to
32 accomplish the transfer as soon as possible to ensure the health and welfare of persons
33 receiving water service in the plantation; and
