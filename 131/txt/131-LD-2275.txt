4 Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, rules adopted by the department to

6 substantive rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.

12SUMMARY
13 This bill provides that all rules adopted by the Department of Marine Resources to 
14 implement decisions of the Atlantic States Marine Fisheries Commission are major 
15 substantive rules.
