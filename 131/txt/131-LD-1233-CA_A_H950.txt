SUMMARY 
This amendment replaces the bill. It changes the Maine State Cemetery Preservation 
Commission from an advisory board under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 
12004-I to an independent advis01y board under Title 5, section 12004-J. It changes the 
duties of the commission from optional to mandat01y. It requires the commission to 
investigate complaints of violations of laws governing burying grounds and cemeteries 
reported to the commission. It permits the commission to hire an executive director to 
carry out the functions of the commission and authorizes the executive director to hire 
additional staff as needed. It requires the commission to develop a budget for each year 
and include the budget, income and expenses and details of the commission's activities in 
a report due to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over state 
and local government matters by December 15th of each year. It establishes the Cemete1y 
Preservation Commission Fund for use by the commission to accomplish the duties of the 
commission. The State Controller is required to deposit into the fund, on a quaiterly basis, 
$5 of the burial-transit permit fee collected by the Depaitment of Health and Human 
Services. It requires the Depa1tment of Health and Human Services to amend its rules to 
increase the fee for a burial-transit permit by $5. The amendment also adds an 
appropriations and allocations section.
