Page 1 - 131LR2939(01)1Sec. 1.  Commission convened; review of traffic fatalities involving OUI.  
2Resolved:  That the Attorney General shall convene a commission with membership made 
3 up of law enforcement officers and district attorneys to review traffic fatalities in the State 
4 in which an operator was charged with OUI, as defined in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 
5 29-A, section 2401, subsection 8. The commission shall consider the enforcement of and 
6 prosecution involving the laws governing OUI offenses in the State, focusing primarily on 
7 OUI offenses that result in death.
8Sec. 2.  Best practices guide; OUI enforcement and prosecution.  Resolved:  
9 That the commission under section 1 shall create a set of best practices to be used as a guide 
10 in the enforcement of and prosecution involving the laws governing OUI offenses in the 
11 State and shall make this guide available to law enforcement agencies and district attorneys 
12 in the State.
13SUMMARY
14 This resolve directs the Attorney General to convene a commission with membership 
15 made up of law enforcement officers and district attorneys to review traffic fatalities in the 
16 State in which an operator was charged with operating under the influence, or OUI, and the 
17 subsequent prosecution of those occurrences.  The commission is directed to create a set of 
18 best practices to be used as a guide in the enforcement of and prosecution involving the 
19 laws governing OUI offenses in the State.14
