29SUMMARY
30 This bill makes technical changes to the State's marine resources laws.  It eliminates 
31 the Aquaculture Monitoring, Research and Development Fund, which is no longer 
32 capitalized.  It clarifies language regarding the requirement for a license holder to have the 
33 license holder's license, elver transaction card and photo identification in the license 
34 holder's possession when engaging in licensed activities.  It also removes an existing 
35 exception to holding a seaweed permit when selling seaweed that has detached naturally 
36 and is dead.30
