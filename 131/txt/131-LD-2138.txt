1Sec. 1. Department of Health and Human Services to raise overnight bed 
2rate for low-barrier shelters. Resolved: That the Department of Health and Human 
3 Services shall increase its overnight bed rate of reimbursement for low-barrier shelters to 
4 $90 per night.  For purposes of this section, "low-barrier shelter" means a shelter that has 
5 no or minimal requirements for entry and does not require a criminal background check, a 
6 credit check, participation in a program, sobriety or identification in order for a person to 
7 stay in the shelter.
8Sec. 2. Department of Health and Human Services to pursue waiver. 
9Resolved: That the Department of Health and Human Services shall pursue a waiver from 
10 the United States Department of Health and Human Services, Centers for Medicare and 
11 Medicaid Services to access Medicaid funds to serve unhoused persons with substance use 
12 disorder or mental health disorders or who are elderly persons with medical needs or 
13 persons with disabilities with medical needs.
14SUMMARY
15 This resolve directs the Department of Health and Human Services to increase its 
16 overnight bed rate of reimbursement for low-barrier shelters to $90 per night.  It also directs 
17 the Department of Health and Human Services to pursue a waiver from the United States 
18 Department of Health and Human Services, Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services 
19 to access Medicaid funds to serve unhoused persons with substance use disorder or mental 
20 health disorders or who are elderly persons with medical needs or persons with disabilities 
21 with medical needs.
