2Sec. 1.  12 MRSA §6310, sub-§1, as amended by PL 2011, c. 266, Pt. A, §3, is 
3 further amended to read:
41.  Appeal of license denial.  A person who is denied a Class I, Class II or Class III 
5 lobster and crab fishing license because that person does not meet the eligibility 
6 requirements of section 6421, subsection 5, paragraph A; a person who is denied a 
7 handfishing sea urchin license, a sea urchin dragging license or a sea urchin hand-raking 
8 and trapping license because that person does not meet the eligibility requirements of 
9 section 6749‑O, subsection 2‑A; or a person who is denied a hand fishing scallop license 
10 or a scallop dragging license because that person does not meet the eligibility requirements 
11 of section 6706, subsection 2; or a person who is denied a resident commercial menhaden 
12 fishing license or a nonresident commercial menhaden fishing license because that person 
13 does not meet the eligibility requirements of section 6502-C, subsection 1-A or 1-B may 
14 appeal to the commissioner under this section for a review of that license denial.
15Sec. 2.  12 MRSA §6310, sub-§2, ¶D is enacted to read:
16 D.  A resident commercial menhaden fishing license or a nonresident commercial 
17 menhaden fishing license may be issued to a person on appeal only if a substantial 
18 illness or medical condition on the part of the person or a family member prevented 
19 that person from meeting the eligibility requirements for that license and the person 
20 documents that the person fished for menhaden while in possession of the same license 
21 within one year prior to the onset of the illness or medical condition.  The person shall 
22 provide the commissioner with documentation from a physician describing the illness 
23 or other medical condition.  A person must request an appeal under this paragraph 
24 within one year of the onset of the illness or medical condition.
25SUMMARY
26 This bill allows a holder of a resident commercial menhaden fishing license or a 
27 nonresident commercial menhaden fishing license to appeal a license denial if a substantial 
28 illness or medical condition on the part of the person or a family member prevented that 
29 person from meeting the eligibility requirements for that license.
