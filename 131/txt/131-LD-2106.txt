1Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2Sec. 1.  36 MRSA §5219-BB, sub-§4, as repealed and replaced by PL 2013, c. 550, 
3 §1 and affected by §2, is amended to read:
44.  Maximum credit.  The credit allowed pursuant to this section and section 2534 
5 may not exceed the greater of:
6 A.  Five million dollars for the portion of a certified rehabilitation as defined by the 
7 Code, Section 47(c)(2)(C) placed in service in the State in the taxable year. For tax 
8 years beginning on or after January 1, 2024, $10,000,000 for the portion of a certified 
9 rehabilitation as defined by the Code, Section 47(c)(2)(C) placed in service in the State 
10 in the taxable year; and
11 B.  Five million dollars for each building that is a component of a certified historic 
12 structure for which a credit is claimed under this section. For tax years beginning on or 
13 after January 1, 2024, $10,000,000 for each building that is a component of a certified 
14 historic structure for which a credit is claimed under this section.
15Sec. 2.  36 MRSA §5256, sub-§4 is enacted to read:
164.  Special rule for nonprofit organizations claiming affordable housing or historic 
17preservation tax credits.  Notwithstanding subsections 1, 2 and 3, for tax years beginning 
18 on or after January 1, 2024, a nonprofit organization that is exempt from taxation under 
19 Section 501(c)(3), Section 501(c)(4) or Section 501(c)(6) of the Code and is claiming 
20 refundable credits under section 5219-BB or 5219-WW may elect the calendar year for 
21 purposes of the tax imposed by this Part and such refundable credits for any year in which 
22 such refundable credits are claimed.
23SUMMARY
24 This bill increases the maximum tax credit allowed for certified historic property 
25 rehabilitation projects. It also allows nonprofit organizations that claim the historic 
26 preservation tax credit or the affordable housing tax credit to file their refund claims on a 
27 calendar year basis.  The changes in the bill apply to tax years beginning on or after January 
28 1, 2024.
