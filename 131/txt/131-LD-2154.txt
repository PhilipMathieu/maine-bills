3SUMMARY
4 This bill makes changes to the laws governing adoption subsidy and child care. The 
5 bill:
6 1.  Removes the requirement of an annual redetermination for adoption assistance;
7 2.  Allows, instead of requires, the Department of Health and Human Services to adopt 
8 rules implementing the First 4 ME Early Care and Education Program; and
9 3.  Allows the department to issue temporary licenses for licensed family child care 
10 providers and child care facilities that need to relocate due to emergency circumstances or 
11 to new family child care providers and child care facilities established due to emergency 
12 circumstances and provides a definition of "emergency circumstances."
