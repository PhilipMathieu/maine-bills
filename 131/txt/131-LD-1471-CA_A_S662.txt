16 SUMMARY
17 This amendment replaces the bill with a resolve and adds an emergency preamble and
18 clause. It provides for legislative review of portions of Chapter 200: Metallic Mineral
19 Exploration, Advanced Exploration and Mining, a maj or substantive rule of the Department
20 of Environmental Protection that was filed outside the legislative rule acceptance period on
21 February 29, 2024. The amendment approves final adoption of the rule only if certain
22 changes are made to the rule, as specified in the amendment, prior to final adoption.
