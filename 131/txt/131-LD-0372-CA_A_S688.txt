SUMMARY 
This amendment, which is the majority repo1i of the committee, replaces the bill, which 
is a concept draft, and changes the title. The amendment allows the Director of the Bureau 
of Labor Standards within the Department of Labor to order an employer, officer, agent or 
other person to pay unpaid wages determined to be due as well as an additional amount 
equal to twice the amount of unpaid wages as liquidated damages and a reasonable rate of 
interest. It requires the Attorney General to institute a civil action to recover any unpaid 
wages, liquidated damages and interest, along with the fine as in current law. It clarifies 
that an employee may not receive payment more than once for the same unpaid wages and 
liquidated damages owed to the employee. It also provides that the Depaiiment of Labor is 
authorized to receive the unpaid wages, liquidated damages and interest on behalf of an 
employee, which the director must pay to the employee.
