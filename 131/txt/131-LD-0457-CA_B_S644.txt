Fiscal Detail and Notes
This legislation provides that, for fiscal year 2024-25 only, the cost-of-living adjustment (COLA) awarded to retired
state employees and teachers and their beneficiaries applies to the first $40,000 of the retirement benefit. According to
the Maine Public Employees Retirement System, this provision will create an unfunded actuarial liability (UAL)
totaling $39,836,000 that, pursuant to the Maine Constitution, will have to be funded immediately. This bill includes a
one-time General Fund appropriation in that amount to the Retirement Allowance Fund within the Maine Public
Employees Retirement System in fiscal year 2024-25 to pay for the cost of the COLA provision.
