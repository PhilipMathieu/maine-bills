6 For the purposes of this subsection, "school" includes a public school, as defined in Title

10 12.
11 Distance must be measured from the main entrance of the retailer's premises to the main 
12 entrance of the school by the ordinary course of travel.
13SUMMARY
14 This bill prohibits the Department of Health and Human Services from issuing a new 
15 or renewed retail tobacco license to a retailer operating within 1,000 feet of a school.14
