1Emergency preamble.  Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 
2 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and
3Whereas, the Town of Perham Select Board does not have the quorum of members 
4 required to carry out the functions of the Select Board; and
5Whereas, without the election of additional members to the Select Board to ensure a 
6 quorum, the functions of the Select Board will not be carried out; and
7Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
8 the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 
9 immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 
10 therefore,
11Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
12Sec. 1. Town of Perham temporary authority of a member of the Select 
13Board; filling vacancies through appointment.  A member of the Town of Perham 
14 Select Board may appoint a municipal clerk, registrar of voters and treasurer to fill existing 
15 vacancies for the purpose of facilitating an election to fill empty seats on the Select Board. 
16 The terms of these appointments end upon the election of a quorum to the Select Board 
17 unless the appointees are later confirmed as otherwise required by law or procedure.
18Sec. 2. Town of Perham authority of a member of the Select Board; 
19authority to call town meetings and elections and designate shortened paper 
20process.  A member of the Town of Perham Select Board may issue a warrant calling a 
21 town meeting and an election to fill the vacancies on the Select Board pursuant to the Maine 
22 Revised Statutes, Title 30-A, sections 2521 and 2528. A member of the Select Board may 
23 also designate a shortened nomination paper process for the election pursuant to Title 30-A, 
24 section 2528, subsection 4, paragraph E and may appoint the necessary number of ballot 
25 clerks to carry out the election pursuant to Title 30-A, section 2528, subsection 8.
26Sec. 3. Town of Perham authority of a member of the Select Board; 
27disbursement warrants.  Until a quorum of members is elected to the Town of Perham 
28 Select Board, a member of the Select Board may approve and sign disbursement warrants 
29 under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 30-A, section 5603, subsection 2.
30Sec. 4. Town of Perham authority of a member of the Select Board; general 
31assistance program administration.  Until a quorum of members is elected to the 
32 Town of Perham Select Board, a member of the Select Board may approve any general 
33 assistance applications and perform any necessary functions to administer the Town of 
34 Perham’s general assistance program that would otherwise require a quorum of members.
35Sec. 5. Repeal.  This Act is repealed December 31, 2024.
36Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
37 takes effect when approved.
38SUMMARY
39 This bill allows a member of the Town of Perham Select Board to take action to 
40 facilitate an election to fill empty seats on the Select Board. Until a quorum of members is
