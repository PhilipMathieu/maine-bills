Fiscal Detail and Notes
The bill includes a one-time General Fund appropriation to the Department of Health and Human Services of
$3,639,021 in fiscal year 2024-25 for required technology upgrades.
This bill also changes the way child care providers are reimbursed to being based on enrollment rather than
attendance. This change will have an annual impact of $11,610,663 beginning in fiscal year 2025-26.
