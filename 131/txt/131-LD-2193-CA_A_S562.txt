26 SUMMARY
27 This amendment replaces the bill. It provides that the Public Utilities Commission may
28 designate any provider or reseller of commercial mobile radio service as an eligible
29 telecommunications carrier for purposes of receiving federal universal service support and
30 offering services supported by federal universal service support mechanisms, The
31 commission may adopt routine technical rules to implement these provisions.
