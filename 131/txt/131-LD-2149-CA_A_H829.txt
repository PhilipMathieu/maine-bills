This amendment is the minority rep011 of the committee. The amendment clarifies the 
provisions concerning certain labor laws to specify that an employee does not include a 
person who is a seasonal volunteer ski patroller or a seasonal volunteer ski ambassador or 
host at a ski area. It also adds definitions for "seasonal volunteer ski ambassador 01· host at
