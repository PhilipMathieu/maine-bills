20SUMMARY
21 This bill requires the Commissioner of Education to annually determine the total cost 
22 for providing essential programs and services for the Maine School of Science and 
23 Mathematics for students from this State in the same manner that total costs for providing 
24 essential programs and services for school administrative units are determined under the 
25 essential programs and services school funding formula.  For each fiscal year, the 
26 commissioner must include that amount in the commissioner's funding levels 
27 recommendation submitted to the Governor and the Department of Administrative and 
28 Financial Services, Bureau of the Budget and the Governor must include that funding level 
29 in the Governor's annual recommendation for funding levels.
