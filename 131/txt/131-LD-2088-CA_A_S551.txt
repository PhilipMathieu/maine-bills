31 SUMMARY
32 This amendment authorizes the Department of Administrative and Financial Services,
33 Bureau of Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery Operations to issue one additional agency
34 liquor store license to any municipality instead of to only a municipality with a population
35 of less than 15,000 as in the bill. In detennining whether to issue an additional license, the
3 6 bureau may consider any information provided by the municipality requesting an additional
37 agency liquor store license. The amendment directs the bureau to convene a stakeholder
38 group to examine how agency liquor stores are licensed and operated in order to better
39 understand how they are distributed throughout the State and to evaluate the effectiveness
40 of the current system for consumers, the general public, local law enforcement officials,
41 municipalities, the State and entities licensed in this State for the sale of liquor.
