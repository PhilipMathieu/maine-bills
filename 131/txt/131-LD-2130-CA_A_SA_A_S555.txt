27 SUMMARY
28 This amendment makes the following changes to the bill as amended by Committee
29 Amendment "A."
30 1. It changes the defined term "civil disorder" to "organized violence," increases from
31 2 to 5 persons the minimum size of the group engaging in organized violence before the
32 definition applies and removes damage to property as a factor in organized violence.
33 2. It specifies that, in order for the prohibitions in this legislation to apply, the
34 techniques being taught must be intended to cause injmy or death of another person, instead
35 of being capable of causing injury or death.
36 3. It changes the violation from a Class D crime to a civil violation and provides that

Correctional and Judicial Impact Statements
This bill changes a Class D crime to a civil violation. A reduction in fine revenue will decrease General Fund and
dedicated revenue by minor amounts.
Fiscal Detail and Notes
This bill includes ongoing General Fund appropriations to the Judicial Department of $5,000 per year to pay
reasonable costs and expenses of court-appointed attorneys to represent indigent persons charged with engaging in
unauthorized paramilitary training and related adminstrative costs.
