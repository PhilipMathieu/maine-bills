18 SUMMARY
19 This amendment replaces the bill. It authorizes the Department of Environmental
20 Protection to issue a stop-work order whenever the Commissioner of Environmental
21 Protection finds that an activity is being performed in a manner that violates state
22 environmental protection laws or rules or the terms or conditions of a license, pennit or
23 order issued by the Board of Environmental Protection or the department and that the
24 activity is creating an immediate and substantial adverse impact to a protected natural
25 resource, The amendment provides similar enforcement authority to the Maine Land Use
26 Planning Commission with respect to activities under its jurisdiction that are creating an
27 immediate and substantial adverse impact to a protected natural resource.
