SUMMARY 
This amendment eliminates funding for grants to school administrative units to expand 
or implement financial literacy, civics or other system of learning results curricula and 
reduces funding to suppo11 the work of the African American and Wabanaki Studies 
Advisory Council as well as funding for grants to school administrative units to expand or 
implement an African American or Wabanaki studies curriculum.

This amendment reduces the one-time General Fund appropriations in the bill by $2,610,889 in fiscal year 2024-25 to 
the Innovative Teaching and Learning program within the Department of Education. Of this amount, one-time General 
Fund appropriations to support the work of the African American and Wabanaki Studies Advisory Council and to 
administer and provide grants to school administrative units (SA Us) to expand or implement African American and 
Wabanaki Studies curricula is reduced by $1,610,889 and the one-time General Fund appropriation of $1,000,000 to 
provide grants to SAUs to expand or implement financial literacy, civics or other Maine Learning Results cun-icula is 
eliminated.
