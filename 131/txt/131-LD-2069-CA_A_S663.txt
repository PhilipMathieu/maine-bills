16 SUMMARY
17 This amendment changes the ownership interest disclosure threshold from 5% or
18 greater, as provided in the bill, to 10% or greater in the person for which a license or
19 certificate of approval is sought. It also amends the bill to require the applicant for a license
20 or certificate of approval to disclose any person that holds an indirect financial interest in
21 the person for which a license or certificate of approval is sought, rather than any person
22 with any direct or indirect interest in that person, and replaces the definition of interest"
23 provided in the bill with a definition of "indirect financial interest."
