13 This bill increases the maximum amount of securities that may be issued by the Maine 
14 Governmental Facilities Authority for specific allocation to the judicial branch. The 
15 increase would be used to fund projects for court facilities in Androscoggin, Hancock and 
16 Somerset counties.
