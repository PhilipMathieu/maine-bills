Fiscal Detail and Notes
Allowing members of certain boards and commissions to be compensated for their time by the state department or
agency associated with the board or commission may increase costs to the affected department or agency, To the
extent these provisions apply to non-state entities, those entities may also incur costs for payment of compensation.
