15 SUMMARY
16 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, removes the provisions
17 of the bill other than those requiring a competitive electricity provider to provide customers,
18 before entering into a contract and upon renewal of a contract for generation service, with
19 the publicly accessible website addresses and the telephone numbers of the Public Utilities
20 Commission and the Office of the Public Advocate from which the customers can obtain
21 information with which to compare the standard-offer service rate and the rates of other
22 competitive electricity providers; prohibiting a competitive electricity provider from
23 renewing a contract for generation service without the express consent of the residential
24 consumer and establishing requirements for information that must be provided to a
25 residential consumer prior to securing such express consent; and requiring an entity
26 providing electric billing and metering services to provide on each bill the publicly
27 accessible website addresses and the telephone numbers of the commission and the Office
28 of the Public Advocate from which the residential consumer can obtain information with
29 which to compare the rates for service.
