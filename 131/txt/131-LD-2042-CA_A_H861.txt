Fiscal Note
Current biennium cost increase - General Fund
Fiscal Detail and Notes
Reimbursing parents for the transportation of elementary school children residing in the unorganized territory will
increase the cost to the Education in the Unorganized Territory (BUT) program within the Department of Education
beginning in fiscal year 2024-25, According to the department, sufficient funds exist within the program's current
appropriation for the 2024-25 fiscal year to support the cost of this provision, estimated to be $64,400, and additional
General Fund appropriations will not be required at this time, Whether or not additional General Fund appropriations
will be needed in future biennia will depend on the number of students residing in the unorganized territory that need
to have their parents provide transportation, the number of miles traveled each day and the mileage reimbursement
rate,
General Fund expenditures from the BUT program are fully reimbursed by the Unorganized Territory Education and
Services Fund, The expenditures incurred from this bill are not expected to impact the amount of taxes collected
th-ough the Unorgauized Ten-itory Education and Services Tax, which is levied on all nonexempt real and personal
property located in the Unorganized Territory Tax District, in fiscal year 2024-25. The impact of this provision on
taxpayers in the unorganized territory in future biennia cannot be determined at this time,
