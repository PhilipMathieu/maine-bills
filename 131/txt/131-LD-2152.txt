18SUMMARY
19 This bill allows members of boards and commissions currently excluded from the 
20 Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 379 to be compensated for their time.  Current law 
21 allows such boards and commissions to be reimbursed for expenses.  Compensation is 
22 determined by the department or agency associated with the board or commission and is 
23 contingent on the associated department or agency having sufficient funds.19
