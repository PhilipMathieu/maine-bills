10 SUMMARY
11 This amendment replaces the bill with a resolve and adds an emergency preamble and
12 an emergency clause. The amendment directs the Commissioner of Agriculture,
13 Conservation and Forestry to convene the Task Force to Support Commercial Daily Farms
14 in the State. The amendment directs the task force to review and provide recommendations
15 on potential investments and policies to support the long-term sustainability and resiliency
16 of the State's daily sector. The task force is required to submit, no later than January 6,
17 2025, a report that includes its findings and recommendations to the joint standing
18 committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over agricultural matters. The joint
19 standing committee is authorized to report out a bill to the 132nd Legislature in 2025 based
20 on the recommendations of the task force.
