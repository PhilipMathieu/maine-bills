9 SUMMARY
10 This amendment replaces the bill, which is a concept draft, with a resolve. The
11 amendment requires the Public Utilities Commission to adopt rules governing transmission
12 and distribution utility and gas utility terminations and disconnections of service to
13 residential customers.for unpaid utility bills.
14 1, The rules must prohibit the termination or disconnection of electric and gas utility
15 service durmg extreme weather or temperature conditions, including extreme heat or
16 humidity, and establish a threshold dollar amount greater than $50 owed by a customer
17 who does not pay or make a payment arrangement on an undisputed overdue utility bill at
18 or above which the utility may terminate or disconnect utility service.
19 2. The rules must prohibit a transmission and distribution utility from charging
20 restoration or reconnection fees or late fees or requiring a security deposit in connection
21 with the restoration of service if the customer is a low-income customer.
