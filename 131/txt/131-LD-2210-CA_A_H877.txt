Fiscal Detail and Notes
This bill creates a civil rights unit within the Office of the Attorney General to promote and advance protections
under the Maine Civil Rights Act. The bill includes ongoing General Fund appropriations to the Office of the
Attorney General of $193,594 in fiscal year 2024-25 for one Assistant Attorney General position and one Paralegal
position beginning September 1, 2024 to coordinate cases, education, outreach and reporting related to the Maine
Civil Rights Act.
