Fiscal Detail and Notes
This bill includes an appropriation of $ 131,762 in fiscal year 2024-25 to the Legislature for the costs of one
Legislative Analyst position and related All other costs to prepare fossil fuel emission impact statements, The bill
also includes a one-time appropriation of $20,000 in fiscal year 2024-25 to the Legislature to contract with an outside
entity to assist in the establishment of the Legislature's fossil fuel emission impact statement process, Any additional
costs to the Department of Transportation and the Department of Environmental Protection are assumed initially to be
minor and absorbed within existing budgeted resources but additional resources may be needed in the future
depending on the data and modeling needs of the new fossil fuel emission impact statement process,
