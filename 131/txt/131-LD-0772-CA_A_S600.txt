1 SUMMARY
2 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. The amendment provides that
3 a municipality or municipal reviewing authority may not apply a land use ordinance
4 retroactively with regard to a permit application for a development that includes residential
5 housing if the ordinance was proposed after the permit was submitted and deemed complete
6 for processing.
