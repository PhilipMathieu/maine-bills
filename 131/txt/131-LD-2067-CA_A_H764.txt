14 SUMMARY
15 This amendment updates the emergency preamble, changes the title and replaces the
16 bill. The amendment removes the provision of the bill that indefinitely continues the
17 arrearage management program. Instead, it extends the repeal date for the arrearage
18 management program from September 30, 2024 to September 30, 2028. The amendment
19 also changes the requirements for the Public Utilities Commission's report on the
20 effectiveness of the arrearage management program and changes the reporting date to no
21 later than January 28, 2028.
