14Sec. 1. Adoption. Resolved: That final adoption of Chapter 213: Rules for the 
15 Salmonella Enteritidis Risk Reduction and Surveillance Program for Commercial Egg-type 
16 Flocks, a provisionally adopted major substantive rule of the Department of Agriculture,

18 to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A outside the legislative

20Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation
