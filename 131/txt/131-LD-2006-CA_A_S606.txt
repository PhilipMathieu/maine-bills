11 Amend the bill by insertmg after the title and before the enacting clause the following:
12 'Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not
13 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and
14 Whereas, municipalities will be evaluating their resources and preparing their budgets
15 sooner than 90 days after adjournment of the Second Regular Session of the 131st
16 Legislature; and
17 Whereas, enactment of this bill may result in the availability of additional revenue
18 for some municipalities that qualify for a sudden and severe disruption of valuation
19 adjustment; and
20 Whereas, it is necessary to maximize the ability of municipalities to consider the
21 availability of additional revenue when preparing their budgets; and
22 Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within
23 the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as
24 immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now,
25 therefore,'
26 Amend the bill by insertmg after section 2 the following:
27 'See. 3. Application. This Act applies to adjustments for revenue sharing years
28 beginning on or after July 1, 2024 or the effective date of this Act, whichever is later.'
29 Amend the bill by adding before the summary the following:
30 'Emergency clause. In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation
31 takes effect when approved.'
