This amendment reduces the General Fund appropriations in the bill by $175,000 is fiscal year 2024-25 only. As 
amended, this bill includes General Fund appropriations totaling $475,000 in fiscal year 2024-25 to the General 
Purpose Aid for Local Schools program within the Department of Education to provide funding for the Maine School 
of Science and Mathematics. Of that amount, $250,000 is appropriated to support the administrative operations of the 
school and $225,000 is appropriated for financial support to in-state students who demonstrate a need for room and 
board expenses.
