6 SUMMARY
7 This amendment removes provisions from the bill that increase the homestead property
8 tax exemption for a homestead with an assessed value of less than $100,000 and that
9 include the owner's residence within a parcel of working waterfront property. The
10 amendment also removes the emergency provisions from the bill and reinstitutes a
11 provision of law repealed in 2012 that requires a biennial report of the State Tax Assessor
12 to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over taxation matters
13 containing various information about the value of working waterfront land and other
14 information useful for administration of the law, The report may also include
15 recommendations for improving the laws related to current use valuation of working
16 waterfront property,
