SUMMARY 
This amendment transfers $160,575 from the Department of Administrative and 
Financial Services, Medical Use of Cannabis Fund program, Other Special Revenue Funds 
account to the Department of the Attorney General, Human Services Division program, 
Other Special Revenue Funds account and establishes one part-time, limited-period 
Assistant Attorney General position dedicated to the health inspection program within the 
Department of Health and Human Services, Maine Center for Disease Control and 
Prevention beginning September 1, 2024 and ending June 20, 2026.
