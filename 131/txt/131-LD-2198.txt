5SUMMARY
6 This bill removes the exclusion from the definition of "retail sale" for the purposes of 
7 the sales and use tax laws for the sale, to a person engaged in the business of renting 
8 automobiles, of automobiles, integral parts of automobiles or accessories to automobiles, 
9 for rental or for use in an automobile rented for a period of less than one year.6
