1Emergency preamble.  Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not 
2 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and
3Whereas, of the 18 voting members of the Emergency Medical Services' Board that 
4 are subject to appointment by the Governor, there are 6 vacancies and 5 members whose 
5 terms have expired; and
6Whereas, the work of the Emergency Medical Services' Board is critical to the 
7 provision of emergency medical services in the State; and
8Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within 
9 the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as 
10 immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now, 
11 therefore, be it
12Sec. 1. Appointment. Resolved: That, within 30 days after the effective date of this 
13 resolve, in accordance with the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 32, section 88, subsection 1, 
14 paragraph A, the Governor shall appoint members to the Emergency Medical Services' 
15 Board to fill all vacancies and replace those members whose terms have expired.
16Emergency clause.  In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation 
17 takes effect when approved.
18SUMMARY
19 This resolve requires the Governor to appoint members to the Emergency Medical 
20 Services' Board to fill all vacancies and replace those members whose terms have expired 
21 within 30 days of the effective date of the resolve.
