30SUMMARY
31 This bill is reported out by the Joint Standing Committee on Innovation, Development, 
32 Economic Advancement and Business pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 3, 
33 section 955, subsection 4. It implements the recommendations of the committee resulting 
34 from its review of the Department of Professional and Financial Regulation under the State 
35 Government Evaluation Act. The bill establishes the position of Deputy Commissioner 
36 within the Department of Professional and Financial Regulation and provides emergency 
37 authority to the Commissioner of Professional and Financial Regulation to take certain 
38 actions when a state of emergency is declared.  The position of Deputy Commissioner 
39 replaces the position of Assistant to the Commissioner.
40 The committee has not taken a position on the substance of this bill.  By reporting this 
41 bill out, the committee is not suggesting and does not intend to suggest that it agrees or 
42 disagrees with any aspect of this bill.  The committee is reporting the bill out for the sole 
43 purpose of having a bill printed that can be referred to the committee for an appropriate 31
