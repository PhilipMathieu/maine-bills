14SUMMARY
15 This bill increases from 2 to 5 the number of moose hunting permits that may be issued 
16 to nonprofit organizations that provide hunting and fishing adventures to children with life-
17 threatening, critical or terminal illnesses and specifies that at least 3 of the permits must be 
18 issued to an organization based in the State and given to children who are residents of the 
19 State.
