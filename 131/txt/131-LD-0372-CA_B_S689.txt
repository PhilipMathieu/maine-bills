3 SUMMARY 
4 This amendment, which is the minority rep01t of the committee, replaces the bill, which 
5 is a concept draft, and changes the title. The amendment allows the Director of the Bureau 
6 of Labor Standards within the Depaitment of Labor, if the director finds that a violation 
7 was willful, to order an employer, officer, agent or other person to pay unpaid wages 
8 determined to be due, as well as an additional amount equal to the amount of unpaid wages 
9 as liquidated damages and a reasonable rate of interest. It requires the Attorney General to 
10 institute a civil action to recover any unpaid wages, liquidated damages and interest, along 
11 with the fine as in current law. It also provides that the Depaiiment of Labor is authorized 
12 to receive the unpaid wages, liquidated damages and interest on behalf of an employee, 
13 which the director must pay to the employee.
