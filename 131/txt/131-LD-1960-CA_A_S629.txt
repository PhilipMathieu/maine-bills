6 SUMMARY
7 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, replaces the bill, which
8 is a concept draft, and changes the title. It amends the law governing the presence of
9 perfluoroalkyl and polyfluoroalkyl substances in products to exempt from the requirements
10 of that law a pesticide or spray adjuvant the application of which is regulated by or under
11 the jurisdiction of the United States Food and Dmg Admmistration, the United States
12 Department of Agriculture or the United States Environmental Protection Agency pursuant
13 to the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act.
