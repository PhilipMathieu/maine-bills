31SUMMARY
32 This bill amends the laws authorizing MaineCare member copayments. The bill 
33 removes references to certain service categories subject to copayments, prohibits 
34 copayments for community-based behavioral health and primary care services, clarifies the 
35 list of service categories subject to copayments and provides the Department of Health and 
36 Human Services authority to set copayment amounts.
