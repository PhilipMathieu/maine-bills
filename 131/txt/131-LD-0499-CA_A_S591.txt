16 §600-C. Pretax commuter benefit 
17 Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, beginning July 1, 2025, an 
18 employer of more than 20 employees in the State shall, in accordance with rules adopted 
19 by the Depattment of Labor in consultation with the State Tax Assessor, offer to those 
20 employees a pretax commuter benefit. For purposes of this section, "pretax commuter 
21 benefit" means earnings of an employee up to $300 per month, set aside by the employer 
22 at the request of the employee, to be used for transpottation expenses related to the 
23 employee's job, including, but not limited to, commuting to and from work. Rules adopted 
24 pursuant to this section are routine technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, 
25 subchapter 2-A.'
