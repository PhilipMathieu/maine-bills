14 SUMMARY
15 This amendment amends Committee Amendment "A" and places the same restrictions
16 on rent increases for mobile home park owners or operators as are placed on landlords of
17 residential estates. This amendment also provides that a mobile home park owner or
18 operator may not require an applicant to pay a fee to submit an application to enter into an
19 agreement for rental of a mobile home or mobile home park lot, except that a mobile home
20 park owner or operator may require an applicant to pay the actual cost of a background
21 check, a credit check or another screening process. The amendment requires the mobile
22 home park owner or operator to provide the applicant with any information obtained
23 pursuant to a background check, credit check or other screening process. The amendment
24 also prohibits a mobile home park owner or operator from charging more than one fee for
25 a background check, credit checlcor other screening process in any 12-month period,
