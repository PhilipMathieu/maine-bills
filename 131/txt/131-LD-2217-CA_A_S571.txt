Fiscal Detail and Notes
The bill includes Other Special Revenue Funds allocations of $3,210,240 in fiscal year 2024-25 to the Department of
Health and Human Services for payment to the Maine Veterans' Homes to support ongoing operations. The bill also
requires a transfer from the General Fund to the Department of Health and Human Services, Nursing Facilities-MVH
Other Special Revenue Funds account to fund the allocation for payment to the Maine Veterans' Homes to support
ongoing operations. The transfer amount must be increased annually by the increase in the cost of living.
