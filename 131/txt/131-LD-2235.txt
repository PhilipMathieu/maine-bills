1Sec. 1.  Department of Administrative and Financial Services, State Court 
2Administrator and Executive Director of Legislative Council to amend printed 
3forms. Resolved:  That, within existing resources, the Department of Administrative and 
4 Financial Services, in coordination with all executive branch departments, agencies, 
5 offices, boards and commissions and all quasi-independent agencies, boards, commissions, 
6 authorities and institutions in the State, and the State Court Administrator and the Executive 
7 Director of the Legislative Council shall, by December 31, 2024, add an option to designate 
8 “X” for gender on all printed forms, applications and other documents used by these entities 
9 that require a person to designate that person’s gender. When reviewing the printed forms, 
10 the departments, the State Court Administrator and the Executive Director of the 
11 Legislative Council shall determine whether the designation of a person’s gender is 
12 necessary for the purposes of each form, and, if it is not, the departments, the State Court 
13 Administrator and the Executive Director of the Legislative Council shall remove the 
14 question from the form.
15Sec. 2.  Department of Administrative and Financial Services, State Court 
16Administrator and Executive Director of Legislative Council to amend digital 
17forms. Resolved:  That, within existing resources, the Department of Administrative and 
18 Financial Services, in coordination with all executive branch departments, agencies, 
19 offices, boards and commissions and all quasi-independent agencies, boards, commissions, 
20 authorities and institutions in the State, and the State Court Administrator and the Executive 
21 Director of the Legislative Council shall add an option to designate “X” for gender on all 
22 digital forms, applications and other documents used by these entities that require a person 
23 to designate that person’s gender. When reviewing the digital forms, the departments, the 
24 State Court Administrator and the Executive Director of the Legislative Council shall 
25 determine whether the designation of a person’s gender is necessary for the purposes of 
26 each form, and, if it is not, the departments, the State Court Administrator and the Executive 
27 Director of the Legislative Council shall remove the question from the form. The 
28 departments, the State Court Administrator and the Executive Director of the Legislative 
29 Council shall make these updates in the regular course of updating their forms.
30SUMMARY
31 This resolve directs the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, the State 
32 Court Administrator and the Executive Director of the Legislative Council to update by 
33 December 31, 2024 all printed forms, applications and other documents that require the 
34 designation of a person's gender to add an option to designate “X” for gender.  The resolve 
35 also directs the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, the State Court 
36 Administrator and the Executive Director of the Legislative Council to update all digital 
37 forms, applications and other documents that require the designation of a person's gender 
38 to add an option to designate “X” for gender in the regular course of updating their forms. 
39 Finally, the resolve directs the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, the 
40 State Court Administrator and the Executive Director of the Legislative Council, in the 
41 course of updating all forms, to determine whether the designation of a person’s gender is 
42 necessary for the purposes of each form, and, if it is not, to remove the question from the 
43 form.
