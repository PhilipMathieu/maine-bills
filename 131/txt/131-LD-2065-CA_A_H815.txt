1 SUMMARY
2 This amendment amends the provisions of the bill and current law to standardize the
3 number of persons required to request a hearing on an aquaculture lease renewal or
4 limited-purpose aquaculture lease application or renewal, setting that number at 10 persons.
5 It also requires that public hearing notices be published at least 30 days before the public
6 hearing.
