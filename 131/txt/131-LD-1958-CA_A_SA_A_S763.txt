This amendment changes the effective dates in the bill from January 1, 2024 to January 1, 2025 and from tax year 
2024 to tax year 2025. Relative to the committee amendment, this amendment eliminates the revenue loss to the 
General Fund and the Local Government Fund in fiscal year 2023-24 and reduces the revenue loss to these funds in 
subsequent years. The amendment also eliminates the $500 Other Special Funds allocation in fiscal year 2023-24.
