COMMITTEE AMENDMENT" A to H.P. 728, L.D . .1156 
SUMMARY 
This amendment clarifies that other qualified subdivisions of State Government 
eligible for funding under the bond issue include executive branch departments and 
agencies; changes the percentage of funds that must be expended in support of 
nonmotorized trails from 30% to 25% and the percentage of funds that must be expended 
in support of motorized trails from 20% to 25%; changes the matching contributions 
requirement from 20% to 10%; and specifies that bond proceeds may not be used to 
dismantle state-owned tracks and ties for nonrail use except as provided for under the State 
Railroad Preservation and Assistance Act.
