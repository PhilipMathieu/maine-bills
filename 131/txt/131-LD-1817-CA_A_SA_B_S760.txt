This amendment eliminates the ongoing General Fund appropriations of $6.2 million per year beginning in fiscal year 
2023-24 to a newly established Outdoor School for All Maine Students Fund within the University of Maine System 
to administer a program to support immersive outdoor education and to assist school administrative units in partnering 
with outdoor education programs and instead provides ongoing Other Special Revenue Funds allocations of $500 per 
year beginning in fiscal year 2023-24 to the same program to authorize the spending of any funds received from public 
or private sources.
