20SUMMARY
21 Public Law 2023, chapter 395, Part A requires motor vehicle liability policies issued 
22 for certain motor vehicles to provide coverage for certain towing and storage charges 
23 incurred as a result of an accident involving the insured vehicle if the vehicle is towed at 
24 the request of a law enforcement officer.  Chapter 395, Part A includes an application 
25 section that provides that those requirements apply to all motor vehicle liability insurance 
26 policies executed, delivered, issued for delivery, continued or renewed in this State on or 
27 after July 1, 2024.  This bill amends the application section to remove the term "continued."21
