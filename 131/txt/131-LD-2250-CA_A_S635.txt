18 SUMMARY
19 This amendment removes the provision of the bill that would have authorized the
20 Department of Corrections to disclose to an alleged victim of sexual misconduct or sexual
21 harassment within a correctional facility information about any subsequent work locations
22 of the individual alleged to have engaged in the sexual misconduct or sexual harassment.
23 The amendment instead authorizes the department to disclose to the alleged victim
24 information regarding whether the individual alleged to have engaged in the sexual
25 misconduct or sexual harassment is still assigned to the same work location where the
26 sexual misconduct or sexual harassment allegedly occurred.
27 The amendment also amends the statutory confidentiality provisions related to
28 personnel records of county employees to allow an alleged victim of sexual misconduct or
29 sexual harassment in a county jail or detention facility to receive the same information
3 0 about the individual alleged to have engaged in the sexual misconduct or sexual harassment
31 that an alleged victim of sexual misconduct or sexual harassment in a correctional facility
32 may receive under the provisions of the bill as amended.
