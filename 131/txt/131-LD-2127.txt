6Sec. 6. Staggered terms; powers and duties.  The commissioners serving on the

8 terms expire and successors are appointed.  Notwithstanding the Maine Revised Statutes, 
9 Title 35-A, section 105, subsection 2, paragraph A, of the 2 additional appointments made

14 the commission but may not vote unless there are fewer than 3 other commissioners present

19SUMMARY
20 This bill increases the membership of the Public Utilities Commission from 3 to 5 
21 members.  The 2 additional members are appointed in 2028 and 2032 to 8-year terms.  The 
22 member appointed in 2028 serves as an alternate commissioner for the first 4 years of that 
23 member's term and during that time is required to attend all meetings of the commission 
24 but without authority to vote unless there are fewer than 3 other members present and is 
25 not eligible to be named chair or acting chair of the commission.1
